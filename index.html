About Me

Nuclear plant operator, turned Freelancer, turned Developer Advocate.

I found Appsmith because I kept hitting limitations in other platforms. They found me because I kept pushing the limits of their platform.

In 2022, I accepted a full-time position as a Senior Developer Advocate with Appsmith, and it has truly been an amazing journey.

Learning and teaching are my true passions, and Appsmith has enabled me to focus more on content creation and engaging with the community.

I’m no longer available for development work, but if you’re looking for training or help troubleshooting a specific issue, feel free to reach out!
