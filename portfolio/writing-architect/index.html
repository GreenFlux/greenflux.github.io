Writing Architect - Michigan State University

Project Overview

In October 2024, I completed a 4 year contract with Michigan State University that was funded by a federal grant to build a data collection and scoring platform for testing student reading comprehension. I worked with a team of researchers to build the app to their specifications, and delivered the project 6 months early and under budget.

Their work has been cited in 4 scientific journals, with my consulting company, GreenFlux, mentioned in the Acknowledgements for programming the platform.
